Latitude vs. Wind Speed (mph)


The graph shows the relationship between City Latitude and Wind Speed in mph for January 5th in 2017. There doesn't seem to be a very strong correlation between City Latitude and Wind Speed. The data is mostly scattered, but there does seem to be a bit of an increase in Wind Speed at the higher and lower end of the latitude range.
